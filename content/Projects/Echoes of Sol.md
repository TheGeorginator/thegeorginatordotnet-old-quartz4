Echoes of Sol (EoS) is a fantasy universe I'm building, inspired by other media such as Star Wars, Star Citizen, D&D, Arcane, and more!

EoS is being worked on in the background, so progress is expected to be slow and with long breaks.

My goal for EoS is to build the universe to the point that I am able to write a story, or run a D&D campaign in universe. 

I'm considering running a D&D one-shot or campaign in order to help build out the universe, and expand the lore.

Expect plan changes as time goes by.
