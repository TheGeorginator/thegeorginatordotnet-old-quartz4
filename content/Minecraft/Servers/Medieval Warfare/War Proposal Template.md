Use this template to declare war on an enemy faction!
___
# Declaration of War:
## **Declaring War Against:**
- A user or Team Name, @mentioning all users involved
## **Goals of the War:**
- (Goals here, such as: "Gaining territory", "Gaining Resources", etc.)
## **The Pot / Collateral:**
- **From Attacker:**
	- A list of assets you're putting at stake in this war
- **From Defender:**
	- A list of assets you're proposing the defender puts at stake in this war
- **Additional Resources:**
	- If there are assets under dispute, that are not owned by either party, they go here
## **War / Battle Area:**
- The proposed areas of the war (The disputed territory, both players' territory, an empty battlefield, etc)
## **Explicitly Protected / Excluded Structures or Areas:**
- Any structures that you or the other party wish to NOT be destroyed, or used in the war, to be designated as NON-TARGETS.
## **Extra Battle / War Guidelines:**
### **Troop / Gear Restrictions, etc:**
- Troop type, gear, and amount restrictions. (Includes siege machinery restrictions)
### **Victory / Defeat Conditions:**
- Any conditions that declare victory for the entire war (Battle specific victory conditions are decided per-battle, before the battle)
### **Additional Conditions:**
- Any additional conditions, such as reparations after the war, surrender conditions, etc.
